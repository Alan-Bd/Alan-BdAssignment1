Software Developer

ABCorp is a well-respected global organisation, with a proud history and strong presence, specialising in secure documents, card services and IT systems across a variety of different industries.

We are seeking an experienced Software Developer to join the Development team, in this newly created role. You will be proficient in configuring Inspire software to deliver bespoke customer communications, provide second level support and develop components for ABCorp’s Customer Communications Management (CCM) platform. As a CCM expert, you will play an active role in our digital transformation journey by helping our clients compose, personalise, format and deliver content.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Work with the Development Manager & Product Owner to develop components for the customer communications platform
  • Identify problems which require additional expertise and take appropriate action including referring problems requiring high level support
  • Provide estimates of development hours based on a SOW
  • Design, create/modify, test and implement software to meet customer requirements
  • Ensure software is developed according to schedule and meets the necessary standards
  • Adhere to established standards and processes
  • Second level support for internal and external customers

    • To be successful, you will need to demonstrate the following:

  • Sound experience in data processing and/or creation of eDM campaigns
  • Experienced in HTML & CSS
  • Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
  • Experience with database programming using either MSSQL, MYSQL or PostgreSQL
  • Unix scripting experience an advantage
  • Experience with web service and system development/solutions architecture an advantage
  • Knowledge/experience with ‘Agile’ development methodology
  • Experience with Quadient suite of products an advantage (full training can be provided for the right candidate)
  • Excellent communications skills
  • Able to work independently and as part of team
  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or relevant industry experience desired

    • At ABCorp, innovative thinking, accountability, continuous improvement, and engaging partnerships are essential in all that we do and provide the opportunity to develop and grow your career with a supportive, friendly and professional organisation.
